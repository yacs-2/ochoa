Book Review Central

The Hobbit

The Hobbit by J.R.R. Tolkien is a fantasy adventure story about Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit who is persuaded by Gandalf the wizard to join a quest to reclaim a lost kingdom.

Animal Farm

Animal Farm by George Orwell is a story about farm animals who rebel against their human oppressor, Farmer Jones. The animals establish a new society based on the principle of "All animals are equal". However, the pigs take over and exploit the other animals, eventually becoming more human-like.

Narrative of the life of Frederick Douglass

"Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass" is a POWERFUL autobiography detailing Frederick Douglass's life as a slave in Maryland, where he endured extreme brutality and hardship, but also managed to teach himself to read and write, ultimately escaping to freedom and becoming a prominent abolitionist by sharing his story to expose the horrors of slavery and advocate for its end; the narrative highlights his personal struggle against oppression and his determination to achieve freedom through literacy and resistance.
