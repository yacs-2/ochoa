Brownies made by G-ma

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
    • Rest butter to room temperature
    • Wash your hands

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    1. In a large mixing bowl, add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cups of brown sugar,and 1 cup of cocoa powder, then mix thoroughly
    2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 teaspoon of baking soda
    3. Set aside

  3. Mix wet ingredients

    1. In a medium mixing bowl, break two large eggs. Whisk until consistent
    2. add 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract and a half teaspoon of salt to the egg mixture
    3. pour a half cup of milk into it

  4. Combine mixtures

    1. Put the large mixing bowl on the stand mixer an put it on the lowest setting
    2. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the large bowl
    3. Then put 8 tablespoons of room temperatue butter in the mix.

  5. Pour and bake

    1. grease 8 by 11 inch pan
    2. pour mix into the bowl the pan
    3. put in oven until desired consistency

  6. Enjoy
